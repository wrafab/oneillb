Week 4


Corel Draw and Using the Laser!


For week four, I learned how to use Corel draw and different laser cutting/engraving technique. After I got my deisgns down, I made sure to hairline what I wanted to vector and left the stuff I wanted to raster alone. To vector means to cut on the laser while raster is to engrave.

What I Made

First, I measured out the clock piece I was using with a Vernier scale. A Vernier scale helped me find the exact width of the clock piece. This helped me fit it properly when cutting my design. First, I took a picture of a bunch of bare trees from a winter day. Then, I got a clip-art design of a snowflake. I made a circle with a diameter of 10 and PowerCliped the trees inside of the circle frame. I took the frame of the circle and selected it. I hairlined it afterwards and started hairlining my snowflakes. I hairlined them so they would be recognized by the laser that they needed to be cut out.
