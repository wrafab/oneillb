CNC Milling

CNC Milling was a bit difficult at first. I first picked a clip art image of a pumpkin, then went to Corel Draw. I turned it into a ____ and put it into Fusion 360. I made sure my mold would be big enough to make a sizeable amount of candy, but still fit the wax.

I set up the machine with Mr. Gerber so that the mill would properly cut my molding wax. I set the origin and the x, y, and z coordinates. After that, I was ready to go mill. We used the _____ Machine and it took about 4 hours to complete. Afterwards, I had to break off the surrounding parts and make a more cardboard surrounding since my wax border was so thin and frail.
